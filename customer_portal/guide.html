Ordering in Four Simple Steps

From login to delivery tracking, follow this workflow to complete every request smoothly.

1

Sign in or register

Use the Login or Profile buttons on the top-right. New customers can quickly register with personal details and preferred customer type.

2

Build your order

Choose container shape, select refill or brand new, adjust quantities, and add optional Wilkins bottles. Your subtotal updates instantly.

3

Confirm delivery details

Set your delivery address, pickup preference, and payment method. Use the default address toggle to autofill saved information.

4

Track progress

After placing an order, open Profile → Order History to watch statuses update, review details, or submit feedback post-delivery.

Quick tips for a smooth experience

  • Keep your browser logged in to avoid re-entering credentials.
  • Check the capacity indicator before submitting large orders.
  • Save multiple addresses by updating your profile after each delivery.
  • Use the feedback form to share concerns or praise the delivery team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to popular portal questions.

How do I reset my password?

Open Profile → Manage Profile, scroll to the password section, and enter your current password along with a new secure one. Confirm to save changes.

Where can I see my pending orders?

Head to Profile → Order History. Pending and approved orders list first, showing totals, statuses, and a shortcut to view more details.

Can I reorder a completed request?

Yes. In the order history table, open the completed order and select Re-order. The portal rebuilds your cart based on the previous items.

How do I submit feedback?

After opening an order from the history view, click Leave Feedback. Rate your experience, add optional comments, and submit to notify the team.
